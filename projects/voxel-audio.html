What it is

Local-first browser audio visualizer and export tool for playable voxel directories, track artwork binding, SoundCloud-style layouts, and deterministic FFmpeg MP4 render kits. This portfolio page exists to translate the live HUB/source route into recruiter-readable proof instead of leaving the work buried in one external index.

Portfolio role

Voxel Audio connects the music catalog to visual/export tooling: audio-reactive directories, artwork-aware track previews, and render-kit generation for release videos and social assets.
