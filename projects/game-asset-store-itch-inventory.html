What it is

Buyer-facing index of public GareBearProductionz itch items grouped by game-dev use case, including live SVG backdrops, procedural generators, VFX tools, sprite engines, icons, sigils, and free lead magnets. This portfolio page exists to translate the live HUB/source route into recruiter-readable proof instead of leaving the work buried in one external index.

Portfolio role

This page closes the portfolio gap around monetizable game-asset tooling. It separates the asset-store catalog from game prototypes, making the commercial creator-product line easier to understand.

Representative inventory lines

  • Live SVG backdrops and scene engines.
  • Procedural VFX generators such as lasers, fireballs, moons, sigils, and projectiles.
  • Sprite and animation metadata tools for combat timelines, fusion, rigging, and export.
  • Free samples and lead magnets that route buyers into larger packs.
