What it is

Canonical start-here discovery list for the GareBear99 / TizWildin audio ecosystem, covering JUCE, VST3/AU, DSP, open-source plugins, FreeEQ8, FreeVox8, and developer visibility routes. This portfolio page exists to translate the live HUB/source route into recruiter-readable proof instead of leaving the work buried in one external index.

Portfolio role

This list gives the audio ecosystem a non-product entry point: developers and producers can discover plugins, open-source DSP references, submission routes, sample packs, and public resources from one curated route.
